OUR BOOKING PROCESS

STEP 1

Fill out the form with details about your event, including date, location, and the artists you'd like to book. Please provide as much information as possible.

STEP 2

Once we receive your request, our team will review it and get in touch to confirm availability, discuss logistics, and finalize the booking terms within 2-3 working days.

STEP 3

Once approved, we will discuss your profile and how you want to be shown on the platform, ready for opportunities to perform at events, festivals, and clubs all over the world!
